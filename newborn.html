Newborn Care: A Beginner’s Guide

Learn the essentials of caring for your newborn baby during the first weeks of life.

Feeding Your Baby

Newborns need to be fed every 2–3 hours. Breastfeeding is recommended, but formula feeding is also an option depending on your situation.

Sleeping Patterns

Babies sleep most of the day, usually 16–18 hours. Always place your baby on their back to sleep for safety.

Hygiene and Bathing

Bathe your baby 2–3 times a week. Keep the umbilical cord area clean and dry until it falls off naturally.

When to Seek Help

Contact a doctor if your baby has a fever, refuses to feed, or shows unusual behavior.
