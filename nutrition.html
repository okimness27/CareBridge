Child Nutrition: Building Healthy Eating Habits

Proper nutrition is essential for your child's growth and development.

Balanced Diet

A balanced diet includes fruits, vegetables, protein, and carbohydrates. This helps your child grow strong and healthy.

Limit Junk Food

Too much junk food can lead to health problems. Try to reduce sugary snacks and fast food.

Encourage Healthy Habits

Teach children to drink water instead of sugary drinks and eat meals regularly.

Be a Role Model

Children copy what they see. Eating healthy as a parent encourages your child to do the same.
